All children, including those with disabilities, need love, respect, nurturing, and time, especially during difficult and uncertain times.

Types of plays.

  • There are many different types of play that can be both fun and Educational
  • Language,numbers,objects,drama,and music games give children opportunities to explore and express themselves in a safe and fun way

Telling stories.

  • Tell your children a story from your own childhood
  • Ask your children to tell you a story
  • Make up a new story together starting with "once upon a time...". Each person adds a new sentence to the story.

Change the Object!

  • Everyday household items like brooms,mops,or scarfs can become fun props for games
  • Place an object in the center of the room and whenever someone has an idea,they jump in and show the rest what the object can be.
  • For example,a broom might become a horse,microphone,or even a guitar!

Movement games

  • Create a dance choreography to your children,s favorite songs.First person does a dance and everyone else copies.Everyone takes turns being the leader.
  • "Challenge" who can do most toe touches,jumping jacks,or laps around the room in a minute.
  • "Mirror" each other,s facial expressions,movements,sounds.one person can start as a leader and then switch.Try it with no leaders.
  • Freeze dance: Play music or someone sings a song and everyone dances.When the music stops,everyone must freeze.Last person still dancing becomes the judge for the next round.
  • Animal dance: Same as above but when the music stops,call out a name of an animal,and everyone has to become that animal.

Memory game.

  • First person says,"when COVID-19 ends, I am going to....(e.g,go to the park)".
  • Second person adds to to the first person,"when COVID-19 ends,I am going to the park and...(e.g,visit my best friend)".
  • You can change this to any scenario in the future(e.g...,when i see my friend).

Singing

  • Singing songs to your baby or a young child helps develop language.
  • Play or sing a song,and the first one to guess it right becomes the next eader.
  • Make up a song about handwashing or physical distancing.Add dance movements!
